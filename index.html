a little about me

My name is Natalya Lazankova. I am professional photographer, freelancer, traveler. I worked for the most famous magazines and fashion houses.

I photograph from an early age. At first it was fun, but later it became a passion. I performed my first „commercial” orders at the age of 15. I have developed a lot in over 10 years. I have traveled many countries and met many great people.

I currently live and work in beautiful Iceland. I am co-creating a project here with an international large travel magazine. I feel great here. You will know the result of my work soon, and now I invite you to visit my instagram account, where you will see how I work every day and how I live in one of the most beautiful places in the world!
